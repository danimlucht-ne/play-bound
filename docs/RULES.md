# 📜 Welcome to the PlayBound Support HQ!

We’re thrilled to have you here! This server is your central hub for all things PlayBound—from getting help and reporting bugs to hanging out with other server owners and climbing the global leaderboards.

To keep this community safe, helpful, and fun for everyone, please read and follow these rules:

## 🤝 1. Be Respectful
*   Treat everyone (players, server owners, and staff) with kindness and respect.
*   Hate speech, harassment, discrimination, or toxic behavior of any kind will result in an immediate and permanent ban.

## 🚫 2. No Spam or Self-Promotion
*   Please do not spam messages, emojis, or bot commands in the general chat channels.
*   Do not advertise your own Discord servers, bots, or services here without explicit permission from the staff.

## 🛑 3. Keep it SFW (Safe for Work)
*   This is a PG-13 community. Absolutely no NSFW (Not Safe For Work) content, including text, images, or links, is allowed anywhere in this server.

## 🤖 4. PlayBound Economy Rules
*   **No Exploiting:** If you find a bug that gives you infinite points, report it immediately via a `/ticket`. Abusing exploits will get you blacklisted from the bot globally.
*   **No Macroing/Automating:** Using external scripts or "auto-typers" to farm points, win Trivia Sprints, or grind the Daily Streak is strictly prohibited and will result in a permanent ban and a point wipe.

## 🛠️ 5. Getting Help & Support
*   **Use the Ticket System:** If you need help, have a suggestion, or found a bug, **do not DM the developers**. Instead, type **`/ticket`** anywhere in this server. The bot will create a private thread just for you and the staff.
*   **Be Patient:** Our staff are real people. We will respond to your ticket as soon as we can!
